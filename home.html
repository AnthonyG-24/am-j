About AM&J Movers & Installation

Based out of Garner, NC, AM&J specializes in stress-free moving services. We are a team of expert movers that specialize in making your move simple and hassle free. Our services include loading, unloading, packing, unpacking, and U-Box container delivery. Whether it's a local move or a cross-country job, from careful packing and furniture moving to appliance installation and long-haul deliveries, our team handles it all with precision and care.

Moving & Packing

Professional residential and commercial moving services. Our team carefully packs and transports your belongings using proven methods that protect everything from start to finish.

Installation & Setup

Expert appliance and furniture installation services for homes and businesses. Quick, clean, and professional work with attention to every detail.

Logistics & Delivery

Reliable delivery routes across the US and worldwide. From local stops to coast-to-coast hauls, we help move freight with confidence and reliability.
