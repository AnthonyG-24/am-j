About AM&J Movers & Installation

AM&J, we’re not just movers. We help people and businesses transition smoothly, whether it’s a local move or a cross-country job. From careful packing and furniture moving to appliance installation and long-haul deliveries, our team handles it all with precision and care.

Furniture Moving & Packing

We take the stress out of moving. Our team packs and transports furniture with care, using proven methods that protect your belongings from start to finish. Whether it’s a local move or world wide shipping, we make sure everything arrives safely and on time.

Appliance Installation & Delivery Routes

Need a new appliance set up? We’ve got it covered. Our technicians handle both home and commercial installations with efficiency and attention to detail. We also manage dependable delivery routes, from local stops to coast-to-coast hauls, helping logistics companies move freight with confidence.
